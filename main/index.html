About Ankara


The Capital of Turkey: Ankara The capital of Turkey, Ankara offers many eye-catching options for both visitors and residents. The city has buildings wit national values such as Anıtkabir and the building of National Assembly of Turkey as well as several important museums. Ethnography Museum and Museum of Anatolian Civilizations are among the must-see places to understand our history. Ankara, always referred to as the civil servant city, attracts attention with its calm city life. The seriousness of being the capital is felt in every street of the city. In addition Ankara is making a whole new face with new projects. Cafes, restaurants and entertainment areas attract the attention of families as well as young people. The Youth Park is one of the popular meeting points of Ankara. Those who want to take a short walk can visit places such as Güvenpark and Ankara Botanic Park. Atatürk Forest Farm continues to be the lungs of the capital. Although away from the sea, the capital offers a different fun to everyone.
